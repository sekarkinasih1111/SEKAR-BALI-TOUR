Day One Hari Pertama

MelukatMelukat

Before the sun climbs too high, you'll walk a quiet trail to an ancient purification site that has stood for centuries. Guided personally by me through ancient prayers, the sacred water washes away what you've been carrying: exhaustion, old grief, patterns that no longer serve you. You won't leave that water the same person who entered it.

Sebelum matahari naik terlalu tinggi, Anda akan menyusuri jalan setapak yang tenang menuju situs penyucian kuno yang telah berdiri selama berabad-abad. Dipandu langsung oleh saya melalui doa-doa kuno, air suci ini membersihkan apa yang selama ini Anda bawa: kelelahan, duka lama, pola-pola yang tak lagi berguna. Anda tidak akan keluar dari air itu sebagai orang yang sama seperti saat masuk.

Day Two Hari Kedua

Temple & Ancient WisdomPura & Kearifan Kuno

You'll step into temples that have stood for centuries, where incense still lingers in ancient stone carvings. This isn't a tour — you'll be guided to truly understand the philosophy of Siwa Sogata, Ardhanaraswari, and the path of the Bujangga: Nusantara wisdom rarely opened to outsiders.

Anda akan melangkah ke pura-pura yang telah berdiri selama berabad-abad, tempat aroma dupa masih membekas di ukiran batu kuno. Ini bukan sekadar tur — Anda akan dibimbing untuk benar-benar memahami filosofi Siwa Sogata, Ardhanaraswari, dan jalan Bujangga: kearifan Nusantara yang jarang dibukakan bagi orang luar.

Day Three Hari Ketiga

Atma Sadhana InitiationInisiasi Atma Sadhana

The peak of the journey. You'll sit with me for a personal Prana Sangkala reading — your own soul map, traced from birth to today. Then, by ritual firelight, with mantra, mudra, and offerings, you undergo the Atma Sadhana initiation — the moment your true soul is activated and a new sankalpa is planted for the life you're walking toward.

Puncak dari perjalanan ini. Anda akan duduk bersama saya untuk pembacaan Prana Sangkala pribadi — peta jiwa Anda sendiri, ditelusuri sejak kelahiran hingga hari ini. Kemudian, dengan cahaya api ritual, mantra, mudra, dan sesajen, Anda akan menjalani inisiasi Atma Sadhana — saat jiwa sejati Anda diaktifkan dan sankalpa baru ditanamkan untuk kehidupan yang sedang Anda jalani.

FAQ

Good to know before you go

We pick up from most hotels and villas across South Bali and Ubud at no extra charge. Let us know your address when booking and we'll confirm your pickup time the day before.

A deposit secures your booking, with the balance payable in cash or transfer on the day of the tour. Details are confirmed with you by WhatsApp after inquiry.

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before your tour. Changes closer to the date are handled case by case — just message us directly.

Every tour on this site can be booked as a private, just-your-group experience. Pricing and pacing adjust accordingly — ask us for a private quote.

Tell us your interests, timeframe, and travel style, and we'll design a route around them — mixing sightseeing, adventure, and signature experiences as you like.

Comfortable shoes, sun protection, a light layer for higher elevations, and modest clothing that covers shoulders and knees for temple visits (sarongs are provided on-site).
